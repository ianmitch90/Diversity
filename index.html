So whats the point?
Just talking about Diversity isn't enough, we as humans have been talking about this for so long we have forgotten we are the same. The aim of these talks, is to draw simularities between diffrent groups of people at diffrent intersections, and compare notes on solutions, fixes, approaches, and more. If we can articulate our experiences and demonstrate our simularities, we can pull tighter as a global community. Great change starts locally first ðŸ˜Ž
